About Me

I'm Jiyad

I'm a freelance content creator from kerala,India.

‍When I was in third grade, my drawing teacher recognized my artistic skills and encouraged me to pursue art. I received training in drawing and watercolor and participated in numerous competitions, winning several awards. However, when it came time to apply for art college, I was unable to get in. Instead, I pursued a degree in multimedia.

With the support of my friends, I began my career in photography and videography. One high school friend gifted me a mobile phone to help me get started, while another friend from higher secondary school generously supported my ideas and provided financial and emotional support as I established myself in this field. I used Lightroom Mobile to edit photos for pocket money, and eventually began working as a content creator for a Dubai-based food company, creating content for their social media marketing.

‍

"i love creating what i imagine, yes!! i always consider the criticism and feedback.."

By the end of 2020 my brother brought me a cam and started my photography journey. I have a passion for traveling and capturing the beauty of nature in my photos, which I share on my social media accounts. As a result of my work being noticed online, I have received numerous opportunities for photoshoots and collaborations, including a recent partnership with a top travel organizing company in Kerala.
At the end of the day if you guys ask me what is your first priority in life , all i can say without any doubt, it's my family.
That's all about me.
Thank you.
