About Me

I'm Jiyad

I'm a freelance content creator from kerala,India.

When i was in third standerd my drawing teacher found the artistic skills inside me and trained me into a good artist.i won so many drawing and
watercolor competitions,later for preparing BFA exam i joined art practicing institution and i learned so much about colors and art theories.But i can't get into art college and i choose BA MULTIMEDIA as my degree.

I started my carrier with the help of my best friends one of my high school friend gifted me a mobile phone , and another friend from higher secondary spend a lot of money and dedication on my ideas. he and my other friends helped me a lot in building a carrier in photography and videography and I'm very thankful to them. at the beginning i used to edit my friends photos for my pocket money.i used lightroom mobile for editing.
In 2020 when we all got stuck in home with the help of pandemic, i started to learn everything about photography videography even designing with the help of youtube.Same time i worked for a dubai based food company as a content creator for their social media marketing.
